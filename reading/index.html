My favourite books that I have read this year are all very interesting. Some books are series books, like the Siege and Storm series, while others are individual books, like "More than This". Some notable mentions are "Personal", by Lee Child and "The Silence of Six" by E.C. Myers. "Personal" is the newest novel in the Jack Reacher series by Lee Child, and it was also one of the most interesting ones. "The Silence of Six" was a different type of book, and it was much like "Snow Crash" by Neil Stephenson, as it was based upon virtual worlds and hacking. All the books that you can see in the mosaic background are the books that I have enjoyed reading the most over the course of the year.
I own the majority of these books, and the ones I don't, I wish I had them. I would buy them if I get the chance to. I also re-read some of these books, as they were all interesting and worth revisiting. They are like good movies. They never get old and boring.
The majority of these books were fast page-turners, which also meant that they did not take too long to finish. On the other hand, the non-fiction books such as "The Psychopath Test" by Jon Ronson took longer than the rest. This was due to me taking notes, and also reading slower to understand the topic better.
